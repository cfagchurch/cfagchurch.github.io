Statement of Faith

  1. THE SCRIPTURES INSPIRED
    1. Section 1. The HOLY SCRIPTURES, both the Old and New Testaments, in the ORIGINAL TEXT, are verbally inspired of God and are the revelation of God to man, the infallible, authoritative rule of faith and conduct. (2 Tim 3:16)
    2. Section 2. There is no PERFECT TRANSLATION of the Holy Scriptures as there are no perfect translators & parallel languages. However, there are ACCURATE & CREDIBLE versions & translations made by the Bible Scholars which members may preferably use – either for DYNAMIC or LITERAL (WORD-for-WORD) Equivalence.
  2. THE ONE TRUE GOD
    1. Section 1. There is only ONE TRUE GOD. (Deuteronomy 6:4). We reject the absolute monotheism and tri-theism doctrine.
    2. Section 2. However, in one Godhood, there are 3 DIFFERENT DISTINCT persons, namely, the FATHER, the SON & the HOLY SPIRIT. (Father (Ephesians 6:4), Son (John 1;1,14; Phil 2:5-8), Holy Spirit (Acts 5:1-5).
  3. THE DEITY OF THE LORD JESUS CHRIST
    1. Section 1. The Lord Jesus Christ is both fully God and fully Man. (Phil 2:5-8)
    2. Section 2. Jesus Christ is the WORD CAME INTO FLESH to be the Savior of the world. (John 1:1,14; Matthew 1:18)
    3. Section 3. He is coming again as the KING OF KINGS & THE LORD OF LORDS. (Rev 17:14)
  4. THE FALL OF MAN
    1. Section 1. Man was created GOOD and, in the IMAGE, & LIKENESS OF GOD. (Gen 1:26-27)
    2. Section 2. However, due to Adam & Eve, ALL MEN BEING A UNIT, has been tainted with the SINFUL NATURE which incurred not only physical death but also spiritual death. (Rom 5:12-21)
  5. THE SALVATION OF MAN
    1. Section 1. Man, apart from God’s grace, is HOPELESS FOR SALVATION FROM THE WRATH OF GOD in the lake of fire. (Rom 8:3; Rev 21:8)
    2. Section 2. Man's ONLY HOPE OF REDEMPTION IS THROUGH THE SHED BLOOD OF JESUS CHRIST, and is a gift of GRACE alone received by FAITH alone in CHRIST JESUS alone. (Eph 1:7; 2:8-9, Titus 3:5)
    3. Section 3. Salvation is of the Lord & from the Lord. Hence, this is SECURE on the FINISHED WORK of Jesus Christ and the very CHARACTER OF GOD. (John 10:26-30, 2 Tim 2:11-13)
    4. Section 4. True Salvation bears the FRUIT OF GOOD WORKS (Eph 2:8-10) and the FRUIT OF THE HOLY SPIRIT (Gal 5:22).
  6. THE ORDINANCES OF THE CHURCH
    1. Section 1. Baptism in Water
      1. The ordinance of baptism BY IMMERSION is commanded by the Holy Scriptures. (Matt 3: 14-17)
      2. Only those WHO REPENT and BELIEVE ON CHRIST AS SAVIOR and LORD are to be baptized. (Acts 2:38; Mark 16:16)
      3. Water Baptism does not save but a very important FIRST STEP OF THE SAVED PERSON FOR EMPOWERED MINISTRY AND SANCTIFIED CHRISTIAN WALK in the Lord. (Titus 3:5; Matthew 3: 14-17)
    2. Section 2. Holy Communion
      1. The Lord's Supper is the symbol expressing our SHARING of THE DIVINE NATURE OF OUR LORD JESUS CHRIST (2 Peter 1:4), a MEMORIAL OF HIS SUFFERING and DEATH (1 Corinthians 11:26), and a PROPHECY OF HIS SECOND COMING (1 Corinthians 11:26) , and is ENJOINED ON ALL BELIEVERS "till He come!"
      2. ONLY THE GENUINE BELIEVERS of Jesus Christ are allowed to PARTAKE the Holy Communion. (1 Cor 11: 27-28)
  7. THE BAPTISM IN THE HOLY SPIRIT
    1. Section 1. All genuine believers are INDWELT by the HOLY SPIRIT upon regeneration. This happen only once. (Acts 1:8; John 14:16-17; 16:13; Rom 8:14)
    2. Section 2. The Baptism of the Holy Spirit means that SPIRIT OF GOD PLACES THE BELIEVER INTO UNION WITH CHRIST & INTO UNION WITH THE BODY OF CHRIST at THE MOMENT OF SALVATION. (Acts 2:1-4;1 Cor 12:12-13)
    3. Section 3. However, THE FILLING OF THE SPIRIT, should happen every day of our lives, that empowers the believers for ministry, holiness and service should be seek everyday of his/her life. (Eph 5:18)
  8. THE GIFTS OF SIGNS & WONDERS
    1. Section 1. Speaking in tongues is also a SPIRITUAL GIFT and shall be EXERCISED BIBLICALLY WITH PRUDENCE IN THE CONGREGATION based on 1 Cor 14:1-40.
    2. Section 2. We do NOT HIGHLIGHT the signs and wonders or any other supernatural manifestation in the Church. However, we do recognize the Holy Spirit to move among us as He chooses.
    3. Section 3. Jesus Christ can choose to manifest Himself in many different supernatural ways to men. It is His sovereign right to do His work with or without supernatural phenomena (1 Cor 12: 4-11).
  9. SANCTIFICATION
    1. Section 1. Sanctification is the WORK OF THE HOLY SPIRIT to the life of a believer which GRADUALLY TRANSFORMS the person into the CONFORMITY TO THE HOLY LIKENESS OF CHRIST. (1 Peter 1:1-2; Rom 8:28-30)
  10. THE CHURCH OF JESUS CHRIST
    1. Section 1. The true Church of Jesus Christ has two kinds, namely, the Universal (Catholic) & Invisible Church and the Local Church.
    2. Section 2. The UNIVERSAL (CATHOLIC) & INDIVISBLE Church of Jesus Christ is composed of all believers of Christ as Lord & Savior across the globe. (Matthew 16:18)
    3. Section 3. The second is the LOCAL & the VISIBLE Church of Jesus composed of all believers who committed allegiance to Christ and to the local church. (Rev 1-3)
  11. THE MINISTRY
    1. Section 1. The Church of Jesus Christ has fourfold purpose:
      1. Evangelization of the world. (Matthew 28:19-20)
      2. Worship of God. (John 4:23-24)
      3. Building a body of saints being perfected in the image of His Son. (Rom 8:28-30)
      4. Meeting human need with ministries of love and compassion. (James 1:27)
  12. DIVINE HEALING
    1. Section 1. Divine Healing is an integral part of the gospel. SPIRITUAL HEALING IS THE ABSOLUTE EFFECT OF THE GOSPEL OF CHRIST. (1 Peter 2:24; James 5:13-20). Physical healing may or may not happen in this life but ultimately it will fully take effect in the Glorification Day.
    2. Section 2. The Divine Physical Healing from sickness is the privilege of all believers ACCORDING TO GOD’S WILL. It is not an absolute right in this life. Only in the Life after Death. (2 Cor 12: 7-10)
  13. THE BLESSED HOPE OF RESURRECTION
    1. Section 1. There will be RESURRECTION OF THE DEAD – both saved & unsaved. (John 5:28-29; 11:25; Rev 20: 4-5)
    2. Section 2. The saved will enter the NEW HEAVEN & NEW EARTH AND THE UNSAVED will be consigned to the lake of fire. (Rev 20:11-15)
  14. THE MILLENNIAL REIGN OF CHRIST
    1. Section 1. The second coming of Christ includes the RAPTURE OF THE SAINTS, which is our blessed hope, followed by the VISIBLE RETURN OF CHRIST WITH HIS SAINTS to REIGN ON EARTH FOR ONE THOUSAND YEARS. (1 Thess 4:13-18; Rev 20)
  15. THE FINAL JUDGMENT
    1. Section 1. There will be a final judgment in which the wicked dead will be raised and judged according to their works. (Rev 20:11-15)
    2. Section 2. Names not found written in the BOOK OF LIFE will be consigned to THE LAKE OF FIRE, which is the SECOND DEATH. (Rev 20:11-15)
  16. THE NEW HEAVEN & NEW EARTH
    1. Section 1. God will make a new heaven & new earth. (Revelation 22)
  17. ADDENDUM
    1. Section 1. We further strongly adhere to:
      1. The Apostle’s Creed;
      2. Biblical Truth & GOD-CENTERED Theology
      3. The 5 SOLAS of REFORMATION
        1. Sola Fide,
        2. Sola Gratia,
        3. Solus Christus,
        4. Sola Scriptura,
        5. Soli Deo Gloria.
      4. We Categorically Reject the Heresies of:
        1. Prosperity Gospel & Poverty Gospel;
        2. G12 Cultic Teaching & Practices;
        3. New Apostolic Reformation (NAR);
        4. Hypercharismatic practices like Laughing in the Spirit and extreme unbiblical pentecostal practices;
        5. Hyper-Faith, Hyper-Grace & MAN-CENTERED Theology.
